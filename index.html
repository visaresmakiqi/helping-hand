About Us

We are 3 girls that wanted to find a better solution for those cases. 75 applications for healing outside the country have been issued. People that live here deal with different health problems and they don’t always have the money and the support they need to get better. They have been addressing this problem in many different ways but they have never come across a solution that is faster and safer in both ways. Because collecting the right amount of money can take a lot of time this website will be very helpful. Every day we see posts on social media, we read it on the paper and on the news at night about people with urgent help need. It shows their photos and personal information like bank accounts, phone number, addresses etc on how you can help them by donating money. Sometimes people don’t believe posts of this nature that they see on different social medias so they hesitate to donate their money. Kosovo is a small place who is in her first steps of development and hospitals can’t provide a cure for every diesis. Hospitals don’t have the support system they need to help all those people. So with the help from our donators everyone who needs it will get the help thy deserve.
